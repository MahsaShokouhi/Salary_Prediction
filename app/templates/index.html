Average Salary for Each Industry and Role

Across all positions, employees working in “Oil” and “Finance” Industries earn highest salaries, followed by “Web”, “Healthcare”, “Auto”, “Service”, and “Education” industries.



Average Salary for Each Major and Role

Employees with “Engineering”, “Business”, and “Math” majors earn highest salaries.



Relative Importance of Factors in Salary Estimation

Job-type (role) is the most important factor in salary prediction, followed by “years of experience”, “miles from metropolis”, “industry”, “major”, and “degree”.
